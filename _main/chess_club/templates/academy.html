What we offer

Private chess lessons for all ages and abilities

    We offer in-person lessons across New York. Our team of expert chess teachers will provide you with personalised chess training. We also offer online lessons for wherever you are in the world, using Zoom and lichess.org.

Private and school chess clubs

    Chess Rising Stars organise termly online and in-person clubs for children. We also run clubs in primary schools across New York. Our coaches are all experienced chess teachers.

Chess camps

    We run in-person camps for children during the school holidays at Brooklyn Primary School. Each child leaves with a badge and a certificate and some will win medals and trophies!
